Day in the Life

7:00 AM - Coffee Procurement

Katie's first critical task. Kisa provides quality control by attempting to knock the mug off the counter.

9:00 AM - Spreadsheet Summit

The daily battle begins. Numbers are crunched, formulas are conquered, and Kisa claims her rightful throne atop the Num Lock key.

11:00 AM - Mandatory Cat Break

Work halts while Kisa conducts her hourly inspection of Katie's typing technique. Very thorough. Very judgmental.

2:00 PM - Lunch & Audit

Katie eats while Kisa audits the meal for any potential treats that should rightfully belong to the feline department.

4:00 PM - Client Calls

Important business discussions, occasionally featuring special guest appearances by a judgmental black cat tail across the webcam.

6:00 PM - Wind Down

Day concludes with Katie reviewing the day's accomplishments while Kisa reviews the optimal sleeping positions for tomorrow's keyboard occupation.
